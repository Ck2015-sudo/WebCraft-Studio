Our Story

Founded in 2015, Kevin's Pastries began as a small family bakery in the heart of Brighton. Kevin, a passionate baker with over 20 years of experience, dreamed of bringing authentic French pastries to the local community.

What started as a humble shop with just three employees has grown into one of Brighton's most beloved bakeries. Despite our growth, we've never forgotten our roots - every pastry is still made by hand using traditional techniques passed down through generations.
